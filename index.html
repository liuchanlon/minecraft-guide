🐉 Mob Encyclopedia

Complete guide to every mob in Minecraft 1.21 — spawn conditions, drops, behavior and combat tips.

Passive Mobs

🐄 Cow

Drops: Beef (1-3), Leather (0-2). Breeding: Wheat. Cooked beef is a top food source. Leather for books and item frames.

🐑 Sheep

Drops: Raw Mutton, Wool. Breeding: Wheat. Shear for up to 3 wool blocks without killing. Wool colors vary by biome.

🐖 Pig

Drops: Raw Porkchop. Breeding: Carrots. Can be ridden with a saddle but speed is slow — best for food.

🐔 Chicken

Drops: Feather (0-2), Raw Chicken. Breeding: Seeds. Lays eggs every 5-10 minutes. Auto chicken farms are easy.

🐰 Rabbit

Drops: Rabbit Hide, Raw Rabbit. Breeding: Dandelions or carrots. Hide crafts into leather. Rare drop: Rabbit's Foot.

🐱 Cat

Taming: Raw Cod/Salmon. Creepers avoid cats. Village cats spawn in villages with a wandering trader.

🐶 Wolf

Taming: Bones. Once tamed, follows and attacks mobs you attack. Health increases to 40.

🎠 Horse

Taming: Right-click repeatedly. Can be armored (leather/iron/diamond/gold) and bred with golden apples.

🐟 Fish

Types: Cod, Salmon, Tropical Fish, Pufferfish. Caught with fishing rod. Tropical fish are decorative.

Neutral Mobs

🦙 Llama

Spits at hostile mobs. Can be tamed and equipped with chests to form a caravan.

🐻 Polar Bear

Spawns in icy biomes. Protective if cubs are nearby.

🐝 Bee

Flies between flowers and pollinates. Aggressive when nest is broken.

🕷️ Spider

Neutral in daylight, hostile at night. Drops string and spider eye. Climbs walls.

🦴 Skeleton Horse

Spawns during thunderstorms. Can be tamed.

🐬 Dolphin

Ocean biomes. Leads you to underwater ruins if fed raw cod.

Hostile Mobs

🧟 Zombie

Drops rotten flesh. 8% spawn with equipment. Villagers become zombie villagers.

💀 Skeleton

Shoots from range. Drops bones and arrows. Burns in daylight.

🕸️ Cave Spider

Mineshafts. Poison attack. Smaller than normal spiders.

👻 Creeper

Explodes when close. Drops gunpowder. Charged creepers drop mob heads.

🪣 Enderman

Attacks when looked at. Drops ender pearls. Teleports. Weak to water.

🔥 Blaze

Nether fortresses. Drops blaze rods. Essential for brewing.

💀 Wither Skeleton

Nether fortresses. Wither effect. Drops coal, bones, rare Wither Skull.

🧙 Witch

Throws splash potions. Drops redstone, glowstone, sugar, gunpowder.

🐌 Slime

Swamp biomes and deepslate layers. Drops slimeballs.

🗿 Pillager

Patrols and outposts. Part of raids. Drops crossbows.

🦇 Phantom

Spawns after 3+ sleepless nights. Drops phantom membrane.

👹 Piglin Brute

Bastion remnants. Always hostile. Toughest non-boss Nether mob.

Bosses

🐉 Ender Dragon

HP: 200. The End. Drops Dragon Egg, ~70 XP. Strategy: Destroy crystals, shoot from range, melee when perching.

💀 Wither

HP: 300 (Java) / 600 (Bedrock). 4 soul sand + 3 skulls. Drops Nether Star. Fight underground.

Enchanting Table Mechanics

🌲 Biomes Guide

Explore every biome in Minecraft 1.21 — unique mobs, structures, resources and survival tips for each environment.

Overworld Biomes

🌲 Forest

Wolves, bees nests, flowers. Oak and birch trees. Good starter biome.

⛰️ Plains

Horses, donkeys, villages. Easy building terrain.

🏜️ Desert

Husks, desert temples, villages. Sand, sandstone, cacti.

⛰️ Mountains

Goats, emerald ore, exposed caves. Best for mining.

🌊 Ocean

Warm/lukewarm/cold/frozen. Ruins, shipwrecks, coral, conduits.

🏜️ Badlands

Surface gold ore, terracotta, red sand. Passive mobs only.

🌳 Taiga

Foxes, wolves, spruce trees, berry bushes, villages.

☕ Swamp

Slimes (night), witches, swamp huts, clay, blue orchids.

❄️ Snowy Tundra

Polar bears, strays, igloos (with basement lab), packed ice.

🍄 Mushroom Fields

No hostile mobs! Mooshrooms, mycelium. Extremely rare.

🌴 Jungle

Ocelots, parrots, pandas, jungle temples, melons, cocoa.

🌸 Cherry Grove

Pink cherry trees, pink petals. Rare. Beautiful building spot.

Nether Biomes

🔥 Nether Wastes

Classic Nether. Piglins, ghasts, magma cubes, endermen.

🌋 Crimson Forest

Hoglins, piglins. Crimson wood. Relatively safe.

🌊 Warped Forest

Endermen only — no hostile mobs! Safe haven.

🪦 Soul Sand Valley

Skeletons, ghasts. Soul sand, soul soil, basalt.

🌋 Basalt Deltas

Magma cubes abundant. Basalt, blackstone.

End Biomes

🏝️ Main End Island

Obsidian pillars, end crystals, Ender Dragon. Exit portal after dragon defeat.

🌌 Outer Islands

Chorus plants, end cities, end ships with elytra. Shulkers.

🔴 Redstone Basics

Learn redstone from the ground up. Components, logic gates, machines and contraptions.

1 redstone tick = 0.1s = 2 game ticks. Repeater default = 1 tick delay.

Core Components

🔴 Redstone Dust

Transmits power 15 blocks. Signal drops 1/block.

🕯️ Redstone Torch

Infinite power. Inverts signal.

🔁 Repeater

Restores full signal. Delays 1-4 ticks.

⚖️ Comparator

Compare (A≥B) or Subtract (A-B).

⬆️ Piston

Pushes 12 blocks. Sticky piston pulls back.

👁️ Observer

1-tick pulse when block in front changes.

Simple Contraptions

🚪 2x2 Door

Two sticky pistons stacked.

🌾 Auto Farm

Observer + piston + water flush.

⬆️ Elevator

Sticky pistons + slime blocks.

🔄 Item Sorter

Hopper + comparator.

⏱️ Timer

Hopper clock or repeater loop.

🔒 Combo Lock

RS latches + comparators.

🏕️ Survival Tips

From your first day to end-game domination — practical survival strategies for Minecraft 1.21.

Day 1 — Survive Your First Night

  1. Punch a tree for 3-4 logs. Craft planks and crafting table.
  2. Make wooden pickaxe → mine 3 stone → stone tools and sword.
  3. Kill sheep for wool (or spider for string) to make a bed.
  4. Collect food: kill animals, break grass for seeds.
  5. Build shelter: dig into a hill or build cobblestone hut. Light with torches.
  6. No bed? Camp in shelter with furnace, wait out the night.

Nether Preparation

Full iron armor minimum (diamond preferred). Blocks (cobblestone). Fire resistance potion. Lighter (flint & steel). Food, torches, pickaxe, shield. Priority: Find fortress (blaze rods), collect gold (barter with piglins), locate bastion for debris.

Pro Combat Tips

🛡️ Shield

Blocks ALL melee damage and 1/3 arrows. Essential.

⚡ Critical Hits

Jump + attack while falling = 1.5x damage.

💧 Water Bucket

Break falls, turn lava to obsidian. Carry always.

🔥 Nether & End Guide

Master the Nether and End dimensions. Fortresses, bastions, ancient debris, dragon fight and end cities.

Nether Guide

🔥 Nether Fortress

Search Z-axis lines. Loot: blaze rods, wither skulls, nether wart, gold.

🏛️ Bastion Remnant

4 types. Loot: gold blocks, ancient debris, netherite template.

💎 Ancient Debris

Best at Y 15. Bed or strip mining. 1-3 per vein.

Dragon Fight Guide

Prep: 12+ Eyes of Ender, full diamond with Blast Protection, Power IV bow, Sharpness V sword, Slow Falling pots, blocks, food. Fight: ① Destroy end crystals ② Shoot while flying ③ Melee when perching ④ Collect egg (piston trick).

Elytra & End Cities

🏰 End City

Shulker shells → shulker boxes. End ship near tallest tower.

🦅 Elytra

End ship item frame. Rockets boost flight. Mending + Unbreaking III essential.
